What Does Vayurakshak Actually Do?
Beyond the specs — here's how the Vayurakshak changes the air in your room and the life inside it.
"From smoky haze to breathable clarity — in under 20 minutes."
— Live Smoke-Box Demonstration, Silicon University
🫁

Removes PM2.5 & PM10 Particles

Class-F filters capture fine dust, smoke, pollen, and airborne particulates that cause respiratory issues — especially critical in Indian cities during peak pollution months.

💨

Rapid Air Turnover for Small Rooms

Optimized for rooms up to 75 m², it cycles and cleans all the air in your room multiple times per hour so you're always breathing fresh air — not recycled stuffy air.

🔧

Hassle-Free Maintenance

Designed for simple upkeep with no complicated servicing. Filter replacements are quick, affordable, and don't need a technician — keeping your running costs genuinely low.

⚡

Runs on Less Power Than a Light Bulb

At just 45W max, Vayurakshak costs less than ₹50/month to run 24/7 — making clean air genuinely affordable for hostel students and budget-conscious households.

📊

Tells You Exactly How Clean Your Air Is

Built-in sensors display live AQI, PM2.5, CO₂, and humidity readings — so you can see the air quality improve in real time after switching it on.

Core Technology
Engineered for Practical Air Cleaning
Vayurakshak is built around a multi-stage filtration system optimized for Indian indoor environments — compact, efficient, and designed to deliver clean air where it's needed most.
🌿

Multi-Stage Filtration

A layered filtration system captures everything from large dust particles down to microscopic pollutants — progressively cleaning the air at every stage.

🔬

High-Grade Filter Media

Standardized, widely available filter media ensures consistent performance and keeps long-term ownership costs low — no brand-locked or imported replacements needed.

📦

Optimized Airflow Design

The internal layout is engineered for maximum air throughput with minimal resistance, so every watt of power translates directly into cleaner room air.

🌀

Powerful, Efficient Fan

A high-performance fan moves large volumes of air through the filter stack quietly and consistently — even at lower speed settings.

🇮🇳

Made in India

Locally assembled using domestically sourced components — designed specifically for Indian power conditions, climate, and pollution patterns.

Intelligence Built In
Smart Features, Simple Life
Vayurakshak isn't just a fan with a filter. It monitors, adapts, and communicates — so you never have to think about air quality again.
📱

PureAir App (iOS & Android)

Control fan speed, view live AQI charts, set schedules, get filter-change reminders, and compare indoor vs outdoor air quality — all from your phone.

🤖

Auto-Purify Mode

When pollution spikes above your set threshold, Vayurakshak automatically ramps up fan speed and sends a phone notification. Clean air without lifting a finger.

🖥️

Digital Display Panel

Onboard LED display shows live AQI, fan speed, filter life remaining, and room humidity at a glance — no app needed for day-to-day use.

🔔

Filter Change Alerts

Tracks cumulative runtime and air quality history to predict exactly when your filter needs replacing — no guessing, no wasted filters.

😴

Sleep Mode (22dB)

Ultra-quiet sleep mode runs the fan at its lowest effective speed. At 22dB it's quieter than a whisper — you won't even know it's on.

🏠

Smart Home Integration

Future roadmap includes native compatibility with Google Home, Amazon Alexa, and local MQTT/Home Assistant setups for full smart home automation.
