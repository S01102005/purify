Core Technology
Engineered for PracticalAir Cleaning
Project VayuRaksha focuses on affordable and efficient air purification using standardized filters, modular components, and high airflow design optimized for student rooms and compact living spaces.
🌿

Pre-Filter

Captures large dust particles before they can clog deeper filter layers extending the overall filter life significantly.

⚡

Locally Manufactured

Built using locally sourced components to reduce cost and improve accessibility for Indian households and students.

🔬

Modular CR-Box Design

Inspired by CR-box air purifier architecture for maximum airflow efficiency with simple construction and easy maintenance.

💡

Class-F Filtration

Uses standardized Class-F filters that are easily available locally and do not require proprietary replacements.

🌀

High Static Exhaust Fan

Designed for rapid air circulation and faster room purification with optimized airflow performance.

Laser Particle Sensor

A precision laser scattering sensor detects PM1.0, PM2.5, and PM10 particles simultaneously. Updates every 2 seconds for truly real-time data.

02

CO₂ Detection

Electrochemical sensors track total volatile organic compounds and CO₂ levels — alerting you before concentrations reach harmful thresholds.

03

Auto-Purify Mode

When pollution spikes above your threshold, Vayurakshak automatically ramps up fan speed and notifies your phone, no manual intervention needed.

View historical AQI charts, schedule purification, control remotely, get filter replacement reminders, and compare outdoor vs indoor air quality.
